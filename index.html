About Me

I am a Research Fellow at Department of Diagnostic Radiology and Nuclear Medicine of University of University of Maryland School of Medicine, supervised by Prof. Wang.I obtained my Master in Clinical Cognitive Neuroscience at CCBD , with Honours, from Hangzhou Normal University (HZNU) and Bachelor in Psychology at School of Pyschology from Jiangxi Normal University(JXNU).

About My Research

Recently,my research interests mainly focus on Brain Entropy (BEN) for resting state fMRI,a complexity measure of brain. Next, I will be dedicated to Social Cognitive Neuroscience. Please visit my Researchgate page for more information.
